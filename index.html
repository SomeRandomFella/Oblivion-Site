Frequently asked questions

Q: How do I import/export webhooks?

Use the Add Shard Group feature in Settings to import. Export by copying from webhooks.json.

Q: How do I add a new theme?

Edit themes.json and restart the app, or use the Custom theme option in Preferences.

Q: What are Parallel and Sequential modes?

Parallel sends pings to multiple shards at once. Sequential sends to one shard at a time and automatically switches to the next when done.
